About Home Sleep Testing

Home sleep testing provides a board certified sleep medicine physician with the information he or she needs to diagnose obstructive sleep apnea. It allows you to sleep at home wearing equipment that collects information about how you breathe during sleep. You will usually set up the testing equipment yourself.

SleepMed Corp. builds fully integrated turnkey sleep assessment programs into your existing medical or dental practice. We use state of art home sleep test monitoring devices to easily screen through a variety of sleep disordered breathing problem in the comfort of your patients home.

Through our expert and experienced sleep network, your overnight home sleep study is scored by our registered polysomnographer, the results are then interpreted by a board certified sleep physician.

Typical wait times in most hospital or private sleep clinics will take about 4-6 months average for a sleep study and consultation by the sleep physician. Some busier sleep labs may take even longer, 6-18 months. Now with our sleep program, patients can now get diagnosed and find out about their sleep quality and whether or not they have sleep disordered breathing within 2 weeks, with all reports leading back to their referring medical practitioner.
